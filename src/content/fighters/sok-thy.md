# Sok Thy — The Spider

**សុខ ធី** is Cambodia's breakout MMA talent, blending a Kun Khmer striking base with Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu submissions. Known for his unorthodox movement and creative striking angles, Sok Thy has become one of the most exciting fighters on the FNF roster.

## Fighting Style

Thy is a rangey counter-striker who uses his length to pick opponents apart from the outside. When the fight hits the ground, his guard game is among the best in Southeast Asian MMA — he holds a brown belt in BJJ under the tutelage of a Gracie Barra affiliate in Siem Reap.

## Career Highlights

- **FNF Lightweight #1 Contender**
- Submission of the Night at FNF: Angkor Rising II (rear-naked choke, R2)
- **7 submission victories** in 18 career wins
- Former Kun Khmer regional champion before transitioning to MMA

## Training

Thy splits his training between Angkor Fight Club in Siem Reap (striking/MMA) and Gracie Barra Cambodia (BJJ). He is known for his analytical approach to fight preparation, studying hours of opponent footage before each bout.

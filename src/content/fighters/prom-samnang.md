# Prom Samnang — The Golden Elbow

**ប្រុម សំណាង** is one of Cambodia's most decorated Kun Khmer fighters, known for his devastating elbow strikes and relentless clinch work. Fighting out of Phnom Penh's legendary Sovannaphum Gym, Samnang has been a fixture of the Friday Night Fights card since its inaugural season.

## Fighting Style

Samnang is a pressure fighter who excels in the clinch. His signature technique — a spinning elbow from the clinch break — has earned him the nickname *"The Golden Elbow."* He combines traditional Kun Khmer footwork with explosive knee strikes, making him dangerous at every range.

## Career Highlights

- **FNF Welterweight Champion** (2024–Present)
- **12-fight win streak** spanning two seasons
- Featured on the main card of FNF: Kingdom Warriors III
- Represented Cambodia at the Southeast Asian Muay Thai Championship

## Training

Samnang trains six days a week at Sovannaphum Gym in Phnom Penh, under the guidance of Kru Vicheka. His regimen includes morning roadwork along the Mekong, technical sparring, and heavy bag sessions focused on elbow combinations.
